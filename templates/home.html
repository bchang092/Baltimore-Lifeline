About Us

Baltimore Lifeline is a volunteer-driven effort to connect residents with critical services. Our interactive map highlights clinics, food resources, and legal aid—curated for clarity and speed.

We believe access should be simple. If you have suggestions or updates, we’d love to hear from you.
