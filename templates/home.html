About Us

Baltimore Lifeline is a volunteer-driven effort to connect residents with critical services. Our interactive map highlights clinics, food resources, and legal aid—curated for clarity and speed.

We believe access should be simple. If you have suggestions or updates, we’d love to hear from you.

Reliability note: our reliability descriptions are based on what we found across calls, forums, online reviews, public listings, and similar sources at the time of review. Services can change, staff can change, and experiences can vary. If you find information that differs from what is shown here, treat our summary as the predominant pattern we found rather than a guarantee of current conditions.
